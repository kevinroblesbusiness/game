# Starry Valley - Gameplay Guide

Complete guide to playing Starry Valley Chapter 2.

## 🎮 Controls

### Movement
- **Arrow Keys** - Move up, down, left, right
- **WASD** - Alternative movement keys

### Interaction
- **T** or **Space** - Talk to NPCs or interact with objects
- **Escape** - Open/close menu

### Tools (Hotkeys)
- **1** - Hand (selected by default)
- **2** - Shield
- **3** - Crystal Staff
- **4** - Journal

## 📊 Game Phases

### Exploration Phase
- Walk around the map
- Talk to NPCs to learn clues
- Collect gems (yellow pentagon shapes)
- Interact with environmental objects

### Boss Fight Phase
- Appears automatically when you reach the boss location
- Use different tools strategically:
  - **Hand**: Can't damage boss, just interact
  - **Shield**: Protect yourself from boss attacks (costs 1 gem to charge)
  - **Crystal Staff**: Attack the boss with star bolts
- Defeat boss to complete level

## 💎 Gem System

- **Finding Gems**: Scattered throughout the map as yellow pentagon shapes
- **Collection**: Walk over them with the Hand tool selected to collect
- **Purpose**: Required for story progression and boss fights
- **Goal**: Collect 5 gems per level to complete objectives

## 👥 NPC Interaction

1. Get close to an NPC (they'll show "TALK" indicator)
2. Press **T** or **Space** to start conversation
3. Choose dialogue options when available
4. NPCs may give you:
   - Story clues (added to Journal)
   - Hints about next objectives
   - Information about boss weaknesses

## 📖 Journal (Tool #4)

The journal keeps track of:
- **Story**: Overview of events so far
- **Clues**: Hints provided by NPCs
- **Objective**: Current mission

Access it anytime by pressing **4** or clicking the journal button.

## 🛡️ Shield Mechanic

- **Activation**: Select Shield (press 2) and press Space
- **Cost**: 1 gem per charge
- **Duration**: 2 seconds of protection
- **Use**: Blocks boss attacks while active
- **Strategy**: Use when boss is about to attack

## ⭐ Crystal Staff

- **Availability**: Unlocked after Level 1
- **Damage**: One-hit attacks on boss
- **Activation**: Select (press 3) and press Space
- **Effect**: Shoots glowing star bolts at enemy

## 💾 Save System

- **Automatic**: Game saves progress after each level completion
- **Storage**: Data stored in browser's local storage
- **Recovery**: If you close and reopen the game, you'll resume where you left off

## 🎯 Level Objectives

### Chapter 2, Level 1
- Explore the starting area
- Talk to all NPCs and gather clues
- Collect 5 gems
- Reach the level exit/boss

### Chapter 2, Level 2-3
- Increasing difficulty
- More complex layouts
- Multiple bosses or tougher enemies
- Requires strategy to complete

## 🏆 Tips & Tricks

1. **Exploration**: Talk to every NPC - they often hint at hidden items
2. **Gems**: Listen to NPC clues about where to find hidden gems
3. **Bosses**: 
   - Learn attack patterns
   - Use shield defensively
   - Charge crystal staff for heavy damage
4. **Stuck?**: Check Journal for current objective and NPC hints

## ❌ Common Issues

### Game Won't Start
- Refresh your browser (F5)
- Try a different browser
- Clear browser cache and reload

### Can't Find Gems
- Explore all areas thoroughly
- Talk to NPCs for hints
- Check the Journal for clues

### Boss Too Hard
- Collect all 5 gems first (gives you resources)
- Study boss attack patterns
- Use shield strategically
